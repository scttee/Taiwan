Bikepacking Taiwan

A moving postcard from the road.

I’m riding my way around Taiwan, collecting sea-spray mornings, mountain-climb days, market dinners, tiny roadside rituals, and the kind of moments that deserve their own little page.

Trip note

The goal is simple: pedal the island, post one honest memory each day, and make it easy for family and friends to dip in.

The concept

Less blog, more travel keepsake.

What I’d do creatively

Build this like an evolving stack of postcards: each day gets a distinct mood, a full-page image, a few handwritten details, and a tiny set of facts so it feels cinematic without being fussy.

What lives here

  • A short diary entry for each day
  • Big hero photos and collage fragments
  • Tracker links for Wahoo / Find My / maps
  • An interactive route map around Taiwan
  • Small details: snacks, climbs, weather, playlists

“The best version of this site is something your parents can check in 30 seconds, while your friends can linger and scroll like a visual diary.”
